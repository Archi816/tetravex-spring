Welcome to Tetravex!

Challenge your brain with this puzzle game. Match tiles by numbers and train your logic!

How to Play

  • Click on two tiles on the board
  • Match the numbers on adjacent tiles.
  • Complete the puzzle to finish the game.

Tips for Winning

  • Start with tiles that have unique numbers.
  • Solve edges and corners first.
  • Stay patient and focus!

Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: 3×3 grid
  • Medium: 4×4 grid
  • Hard: 6×6 grid
  • Impossible: 10×10 grid

Scoring

  • The faster you solve the puzzle, the higher your score.
  • Top scores are displayed in the Leaderboards.

Good luck and have fun! 🎯
